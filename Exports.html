Alabama Car Exports


In 1997, the first M-Class rolled off the Mercedes-Benz production line in Vance, launching the beginning of the automotive industry in Alabama. Today, Alabama has become a key player in automotive manufacturing in the U.S. with vehicle assembly plants for Mercedes-Benz, Honda, Hyundai, an engine assembly plant for Toyota, and a growing automotive supplier network serving OEMs in Alabama and neighboring states. In addition, Mazda Toyota is building a new vehicle assembly plant.

  • Many of the largest automotive suppliers in North America have a presence in Alabama.
  • Alabama is the fifth largest producer of cars and light trucks nationally and a key player in the growing Southeast automotive region.
